Why Give

Your donation keeps the Twin City moving toward zero waste

GreenSphere Impact Foundation relies on public support to run our recycling hubs, pay youth trainees fairly, and supply schools with waste-sorting materials. Every donation goes directly toward equipment, transport and wages for community waste workers.
